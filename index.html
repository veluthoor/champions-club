Methodology
Data sourced from verified check-ins for All Access members active during February 2026 (guest pass plans excluded).
What counts as an activity? Each unique combination of sport + activity type counts as one activity. Examples: "Swimming Coaching" is one activity, "Swimming Games" is another. "Badminton Coaching" and "Squash Coaching" are two separate activities. This applies to all types: coaching sessions, competitions, games, lap swimming, gym workouts, and more.
Key metrics: Sports = unique sports tried. Activity Types = distinct activity formats (coaching, games, etc.) across all sports. Check-ins = total verified sessions attended.
